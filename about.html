Visionary Leader • Entrepreneur • Creative Voice

With over four decades of impact across public service, pharmacy, and community development, Virginia Major stands as a beacon of leadership, integrity, and inspiration. A former Rotary District Governor and John Maxwell-certified Leadership Trainer, she empowers others to lead with purpose and heart. Guided by a lifelong belief in service above self, Virginia devotes her time, talent, and resources to creating meaningful change, mentoring leaders, and uplifting communities. A passionate advocate for youth and women, and a gifted poet and cultural storyteller, she uses creativity and compassion to celebrate Nigerian heritage and inspire a new generation to serve, create, and transform.

Writing & Creative Work

Virginia Major — Poet, Playwright, Cultural Writer

Virginia Major is a published poet, playwright, and cultural writer. She is a proud member of the Association of Nigerian Authors and the Association of Romance Writers of America. Her work spans poetry, plays, songs, and cultural literature. Her creative work complements her leadership and advocacy, reflecting a deep engagement with culture, storytelling, and social impact.

Virginia celebrates Nigerian heritage through her creative works. Honored nationally and internationally for excellence and humanitarian service, she continues to inspire with purpose, passion, and an unwavering belief in the power of leadership and compassion.

Virginia has literary works in progress:

  • River that breaks promises
    This story of love, family, identity, and resilience is emotionally charged. It is a story of devastation, renewal, ancestral legacy, and the enduring human spirit. It portrays the consequences of family secrets, the collision of modernity with ancestral traditions, the endurance of the human spirit, and the resilience required to rebuild a life after devastation.

  • Embers Ignited
    In the heart of London, where the old and the new intertwine, five women from distant corners of the world found themselves bound by destiny. What began as a chance encounter soon blossomed into a sisterhood forged in shared struggles and victories. It is a multi-cultural journey across continents into their world where love is both a refuge and a battlefield, the past is never far behind, and the future is as uncertain as it is full of promise.
